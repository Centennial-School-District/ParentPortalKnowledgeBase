How to Log into ClassLink

Overview

Following our system migration, the login screen and process have changed. Please refer to the image and instructions below to access your account on different devices.

The New Login Screen

When you access the ClassLink portal, you will see the page shown below. You will use the "Sign in with Microsoft" button.

District Computers (Windows/Mac)

  • Open Browser: The browser will automatically open to the ClassLink login page.
  • Click the Button: Click the gray Sign in with Microsoft button (as shown above).
  • No Password Needed: The system will log you in automatically without requiring a password.

Chromebooks

  • Open Browser: Open Google Chrome. The ClassLink page will load automatically.
  • Click the Button: Click the gray Sign in with Microsoft button.
  • Enter Credentials: You must manually enter your email and password (see format below).

iPads

  • Find the App: Locate and open the ClassLink app on your iPad screen.
  • Click the Button: Tap Sign in with Microsoft.
  • Enter Credentials: Enter your email and password (see format below).

Note: You only need to do this once. Future logins will use SSO to log you in automatically.

Student Login Credentials

If prompted to enter a username and password (on Chromebooks or iPads), please use the following format:

Username Structure:

[Grad Year] [Last Name 4] [First Name 2] @stucsd.org

Example: For a student named John Smith graduating in 2038:

  • Username: 38smitjo@stucsd.org
  • Password: Use your standard district password.

Need Help? If you experience trouble logging in, please submit a ticket to the Technology Help Desk.
