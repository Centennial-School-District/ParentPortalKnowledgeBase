Claim Your Parent Portal Account

Step 1: Access the Portal

Go to the updated Parent Portal URL: https://launchpad.classlink.com/csdparent

Note: If you typically access the Parent Portal through the “Family Portal to Skyward/Canvas” button on the CSD website, clicking that button will automatically take you to this updated site!

Step 2: Start Claim Process

Click on the “Claim by account” button.

Step 3: Verify Identity

Enter the appropriate Account details. You will be asked for personal information to help verify your identity in our new system. It is safe to enter this information.

Tip: Click on the eye icon to show or hide the information entered.

  • Email: Enter the email address listed within Skyward.
  • Phone: Enter your primary phone number listed within Skyward. Enter just the number without any dashes.
    (Example: 2151112222)
  • Street Number: Enter only the street number from your address.
    (Example: Enter 48 if you live at 48 Swan Way)
  • Student Birthdate: Enter the birthdate of your oldest student who is enrolled at Centennial. Format: MM/DD/YYYY.
    (Example: 12/01/2010)

Step 4: Security Check

Complete the captcha and click Next.

Step 5: Create Password

Enter and confirm a password, and then click Save.

Password Requirements:
Must be at least 8 characters long, including at least three of the following:
  • 1 number (e.g., 1, 2, 3)
  • 1 special character (e.g., $, #, &, *)
  • 1 uppercase letter
  • 1 lowercase letter

Step 6: Save Username

Copy your username via the copy button, then click the button labeled:

"I have made note of my username and will remember it. (Click here to continue)."

Step 7: Log In

You will be redirected to the login page. Enter (or paste) your username and retype the password you created.

Introduction to your Parent Portal Account

Student Tiles

On your first login, you will be presented with tiles for each of your students.

Student Analytics

When you click on a student, you can view their classes, the apps to which the student has access, and analytics on the apps they are using.

Parent Apps

Below the tiles for your students, you can also view the apps to which you have access, such as Skyward and Canvas.
